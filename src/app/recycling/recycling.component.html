RECYCLING

The product life cycle of an Iphone does not end when you stop using it. It is when it’s recycled it reaches the end of it’s lifeline. By recycling it many parts can be reused, for example valuable metals like gold, copper and tungsten found in the electronic connections of Iphones.

But smartphones are considered a hazardous waste, what do you think is the optimal method to dispose of it ?

SO an ideal production model from start to finish would be a closed loop where the manufacturers would recover the phone in the end of the product life cycle, and use the parts again in the production. It is important to acknowledge, that recycling also costs energy. For the time being it is not energy efficient to recycle all parts of the phone.

To recycle your phone, take it to any tech shop smart phone retailer, they will send it to recycling or you yourself can send it to Apple’s sorting robot, Liam.
